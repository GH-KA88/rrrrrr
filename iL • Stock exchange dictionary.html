What does the abbreviation iL mean?

The abbreviation iL stands for in liquidation. This term is used for companies that are currently being liquidated. This means that outstanding claims are collected, parts of the company, such as machines and land, are sold and outstanding liabilities are settled. The amount of money that remains after the complete liquidation of a company is distributed among the company according to the amount of its shares. A liquidation must be announced in the commercial register. A company that is in the process of liquidation has the abbreviation iL after its company name. IL can include companies, associations, foundations and partnerships.
